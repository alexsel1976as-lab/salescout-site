UK Clearance Deals

SaleScout is a mobile app that aggregates and compares discounted products from UK retailers. Users can browse deals, compare discounts and open the retailer website to complete purchases.

About SaleScout

SaleScout helps shoppers discover clearance deals from major UK retailers in one place. The app highlights discounted products and allows users to compare savings quickly.

How it works

Browse deals

Users browse discounted products from multiple UK retailers.

Compare discounts

Products display original price, current price and savings.

Open retailer

Users tap the deal and are redirected to the retailer website.
