About Clearza

Clearza is a UK clearance-deals app focused on products discounted by 30% or more. It brings live deals together from multiple retailers so users can compare savings quickly and open official retailer websites to complete purchases.

How it works

The app is intentionally simple: discover live deals, compare prices, and continue to the retailer website.

Browse live deals

Users browse 935+ discounted products from multiple UK retailers inside the app.

Compare savings

Each deal highlights current price, old price, discount percentage, and savings.

Open retailer site

Users tap a deal and are redirected to the retailer website to complete the purchase.

Why Clearza?

Clearza is built for shoppers who want quick access to real clearance savings without searching individual retailer websites one by one.

30%+ discounts only

The app focuses on meaningful clearance discounts rather than small price changes.

Multiple categories

Deals cover home, gaming, appliances, watches, sunglasses, arts and crafts, garden, and more.

Direct retailer checkout

Clearza helps users discover deals, then sends them to official retailer websites to buy.

Traffic & user journey

Clearza is a mobile-first discovery platform focused on 30%+ UK clearance deals. Users compare products inside the app and continue to official retailer websites to complete purchases.

User discovery

Users browse clearance deals using search, filters, featured deals, retailer selection, and category selection.

Deal comparison

Each deal shows current price, previous price, discount percentage, and savings so users can compare products quickly.

Retailer click-through

When a user selects a deal, Clearza opens the official retailer website where the purchase is completed.

Important information

  • Clearza does not sell products directly.
  • Purchases are completed on the retailer website.
  • Prices and availability may change.
  • Retailer content and offers are subject to change without notice.

Why retailers benefit

  • Users are specifically looking for 30%+ clearance deals.
  • Retailers receive outbound traffic to official product pages.
  • The app is designed for discovery, not checkout or payment handling.
  • All purchasing decisions remain on official retailer sites.

Current retailer coverage

Clearza currently shows live clearance deals across home, gaming, crafts, watches, sunglasses, appliances, garden, and general merchandise categories.
