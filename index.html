About SaleScout

SaleScout is a mobile app focused on UK clearance deals. The app highlights discounted products so users can compare savings quickly and go directly to the retailer website to complete purchases.

How it works

Browse deals

Users browse discounted products from multiple UK retailers.

Compare savings

Each deal shows current price, original price and discount.

Open retailer site

Users tap a deal and are redirected to the retailer website.

Retail focus

SaleScout focuses on clearance deals across major UK retailers.
