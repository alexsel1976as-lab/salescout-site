UK Clearance Deals in One Place

SaleScout is a mobile app designed to help users discover clearance products from UK retailers with discounts of 30% or more. Users can browse deals, compare savings, and open the retailer website to complete purchases directly.

About SaleScout

SaleScout aggregates publicly available clearance deals from UK retailers and presents them in a simple mobile interface. Users can quickly identify discounted products and navigate directly to retailer websites.

How it works

Browse deals

Users browse discounted products aggregated from multiple UK retailers.

Compare savings

Each product shows current price, previous price, discount percentage and savings.

Open retailer

Users tap a deal and are redirected to the official retailer website to complete the purchase.

Traffic & users

SaleScout is a mobile-first platform. Users discover deals through the mobile application interface and are redirected to official retailer websites to complete purchases.

Mobile discovery

Users search and browse discounted products directly inside the SaleScout mobile app.

Deal comparison

Users compare multiple retailers and identify products with the best discounts.

Retailer traffic

When a user selects a product they are redirected to the retailer website, generating traffic for the retailer.
